Retailer Guide

Easy 5-Step Guide to Place Your Orders

Follow these simple steps to use the Navkar Apotek Android App.

1 Step

Choose Your Role

Open the app and select your role. For retail store owners and stockists, click on the yellow "Customer Login" button to proceed.

2 Step

Access the Dashboard

Once logged in, you will see your personalized dashboard. To search and buy medicines, tap on the "Order" or "Order Generic" grid icon at the top.

You can also track bills, view ledgers, or download invoices from here.

3 Step

Search for Products

Use the top search bar to type the medicine name (e.g., "cipl"). Browse through the available options, check company details, schemes, live stock availability (marked in green), and rates.

4 Step

Enter Quantity & Add to Cart

Tap on the desired medicine to open the ordering pop-up. Type the required quantity (Qty) using the on-screen keypad and click the blue "Add To Cart" button.

5 Step

Review Cart & Submit Order

Open your "Item Cart" to review all selected items, schemes, and the approximate total order value. Once verified, simply click the blue "Submit Order" button at the bottom to finalize your purchase.
