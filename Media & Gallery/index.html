Events & Social Initiatives

Devotion & Harmony

Ganesh Utsav

Ganesh Utsav is a period of profound spiritual rejuvenation at Navkar. We welcome Bappa with immense devotion, uniting our team through traditional aartis, vibrant decorations, and a shared commitment to positivity.

This celebration brings together everyone in the Navkar family, fostering a culture of togetherness and faith as we seek blessings for a prosperous and healthy future for all.

Celebration & Culture

Navkar Utsav

Navkar Utsav is our flagship annual event that celebrates our journey, achievements, and the vibrant culture of our organization. It is a day dedicated to honoring the hard work of our team and the strong bonds we share.

With cultural performances and team recognition, Navkar Utsav stands as a testament to our unity and our shared vision of "Pharmaceutical Excellence".

Social Responsibility

Blood Donation Drive

At Navkar, we believe that our responsibility extends beyond pharmaceutical excellence to the well-being of our community. Our blood donation drives are a testament to our team's commitment to saving lives and supporting the healthcare ecosystem.

This initiative showcases the spirit of compassion and selflessness that defines the Navkar family, proving that we are dedicated to making a real difference in people's lives through every action we take.
